Questions

FAQ

How long will my commission take?▾
Turnaround times are listed per type and are estimates. Actual delivery may vary based on complexity, revision rounds, and current queue length. I'll always communicate if timelines shift.
Can I see the WIP before it's finished?▾
Yes! I always share a sketch stage for approval before moving to colour, and a colour rough before final polish. You're never left in the dark.
What if I don't have a reference sheet?▾
That's totally okay! Written descriptions, mood boards, colour palettes, and inspiration images are all useful. The more detail you provide, the better I can capture your vision.
Do you draw fan art / copyrighted characters?▾
Yes, I'm happy to draw fan art for personal use. Fan art is only available under a personal use license — commercial use of fan art is not something I can offer.
Can I add on extra characters?▾
Yes! Additional characters are typically +25% of the base price each. Complex designs may vary. Ask me in the form or via DM and I'll give you an accurate quote.
Do you offer rush orders?▾
Rush commissions (faster than standard turnaround) are available for a 30% fee, subject to my current availability. Please mention this in your order form.
