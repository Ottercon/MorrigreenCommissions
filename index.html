FAQ

How long will my commission take?▾
Turnaround times are estimates and may vary based on complexity, revisions, and queue length. I'll always let you know if timelines shift.
Can I see WIPs before it's finished?▾
Yes! I share a sketch stage before moving to colour, and a colour rough before final polish. You're never left in the dark.
What if I don't have a reference sheet?▾
That's fine! Written descriptions, mood boards, colour palettes, and inspiration images all work great.
Do you draw fan art / copyrighted characters?▾
Yes, for personal use only. Fan art is not available under a commercial license.
Do you offer rush orders?▾
Rush commissions are available for a +30% fee, subject to availability. Please mention this in your order form.
